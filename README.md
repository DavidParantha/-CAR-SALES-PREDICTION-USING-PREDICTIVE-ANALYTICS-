Car Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Car Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI, aimed at transforming raw automotive sales data into actionable business insights. The dashboard focuses on sales performance, pricing trends, customer preferences, and regional distribution to support data-driven decision-making.

The solution emphasizes clarity, interactivity, and business relevance rather than just visual appeal.

Objectives

Analyze overall car sales performance across multiple years

Identify revenue and volume trends over time

Understand customer preferences such as transmission type and vehicle color

Evaluate state-wise sales contribution and geographic performance

Enable interactive exploration using filters and slicers

Key KPIs

Total Sales: Overall revenue generated from car sales

Average Selling Price: Mean selling price per vehicle

Total Cars Sold: Total number of vehicles sold

Sales Contribution: Share of revenue contribution across dimensions

These KPIs provide a quick executive-level snapshot of business performance.

Dashboard Visuals & Insights
1. Total Sales by Year (Line Chart)

Shows year-wise sales trends

Helps identify growth patterns, peak periods, and decline phases

Useful for long-term performance analysis

2. Total Cars Sold by Transmission (Donut Chart)

Compares Automatic vs Manual transmission sales

Highlights customer preference toward transmission type

Supports product and inventory planning decisions

3. Selling Price by State (Bar Chart)

Displays revenue contribution by state

Identifies high-performing and underperforming regions

Helps prioritize regional sales and marketing strategies

4. Sales by State (Map Visualization)

Provides a geographic view of sales distribution

Enables quick regional comparison across the U.S.

Complements state-level bar analysis

5. Cars Sold by Color (Bar Chart)

Shows popularity of vehicle colors based on sales volume

Supports manufacturing and inventory decisions

Identifies dominant and niche color preferences

Interactivity & Filters

Year Slicer: Analyze performance for specific years

Model Slicer: Drill down into individual vehicle models

Cross-filtering enabled across all visuals for dynamic exploration

Tools & Technologies Used

Power BI Desktop

DAX for KPI calculations and measures

Data Modeling & Relationships

Interactive Visualizations & Slicers

Key Learnings

Designing KPIs aligned with real business questions

Building clean and scalable Power BI data models

Using DAX to create dynamic measures

Balancing visual design with analytical clarity

Understanding how different charts serve different analytical purposes

Some visuals prioritize high-level insight over detailed precision
